How to Play

Goal: Build all four Foundation piles from Ace to King by suit.

Stock & Waste: Click the Stock to deal one card to the Waste. Drag the top Waste card to the Tableau or Foundations.

Tableau: Build in descending order with alternating colors. Only a King (or sequence starting with a King) can fill an empty column.

Foundations: Build each suit Ace → King. Drag any eligible card there to win!
